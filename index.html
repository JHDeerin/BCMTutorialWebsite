The Birth of Memes

In the beginning, God created the Heavens and the Earth.

Immediately afterwards, we came along and were given one job. Did we do it?

No. No we did not.

Anyway, some time passed, God did more stuff among humanity, and humanity - to our utter surprise - continued to be stupid. So, God stepped on to the scene himself to pay for our sins and save everyone who trusts in him.

This, however, only partially solved the whole stupidity thing. And thus, in humanity's wisdom that is below even the fooliest of God's foolishness, memes were born.

God, in his mercy, has not yet punished us for this. He has, in fact, even brought about good from this evil. May we pray he continues to do so.
